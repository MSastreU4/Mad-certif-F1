Fuelgate - 1994 Benetton Fuel Rig Tampering

Summary

Benetton was accused of removing a fuel filter to speed up pit stops, risking safety.

Key Figures Involved Benetton team, FIA officials

Timeline of Events

1994: Jos Verstappen's car catches fire during pit stop; investigation follows.

Consequences

No penalties, but Benetton was criticized for compromising safety.

Legacy

Led to stricter pit stop regulations and safety protocols.
