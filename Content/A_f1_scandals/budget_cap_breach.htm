Budget Cap Breach - 2021 Red Bull Racing

Summary

Red Bull was found to have exceeded the 2021 budget cap, sparking controversy over fairness.

Key Figures Involved Red Bull Racing, FIA, Christian Horner

Timeline of Events

2022: FIA confirms breach; Red Bull fined and given wind tunnel restrictions.

Consequences

Financial penalty and reduced development time.

Legacy

Raised questions about enforcement and transparency of budget cap rules.
