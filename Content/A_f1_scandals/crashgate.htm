Crashgate - 2008 Singapore Grand Prix

Summary

Renault instructed Nelson Piquet Jr. to crash deliberately to benefit teammate Fernando Alonso.

Key Figures Involved Nelson Piquet Jr., Flavio Briatore, Pat Symonds, Fernando Alonso

Timeline of Events

September 2008: Piquet crashes; 2009: Allegations surface; FIA investigates and bans Briatore and Symonds.

Consequences

Renault received a suspended ban; Briatore and Symonds were banned from F1 (later overturned).

Legacy

Raised questions about ethics and team orders in F1.
