BAR Fuel Tank - 2005 Hidden Fuel Scandal

Summary

BAR Honda was found to have a hidden fuel compartment allowing the car to run underweight.

Key Figures Involved BAR Honda, FIA

Timeline of Events

2005: Discovered after San Marino GP; team suspended for two races.

Consequences

Two-race ban; loss of points and credibility.

Legacy

Stricter post-race scrutineering procedures introduced.
