Tyregate - 2005 US Grand Prix

Summary

Michelin teams withdrew from the race due to tire safety concerns, leaving only six cars to compete.

Key Figures Involved Michelin, FIA, Bridgestone teams

Timeline of Events

June 2005: Michelin discovers tire issues; race proceeds with only Bridgestone teams.

Consequences

Fan outrage; damage to F1's reputation in the US.

Legacy

Prompted changes in tire regulations and race contingency planning.
