Double Diffuser - 2009 Brawn GP

Summary

Brawn GP exploited a loophole in diffuser regulations, gaining a major aerodynamic advantage.

Key Figures Involved Brawn GP, FIA, rival teams

Timeline of Events

2009: Rival teams protest; FIA rules diffuser legal.

Consequences

Brawn dominates season; other teams scramble to copy design.

Legacy

Led to clarification of aerodynamic rules and innovation in car design.
