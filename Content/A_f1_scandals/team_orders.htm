Ferrari Team Orders - 2002 Austrian GP

Summary

Rubens Barrichello was ordered to let Michael Schumacher pass for the win.

Key Figures Involved Michael Schumacher, Rubens Barrichello, Ferrari team

Timeline of Events

May 2002: Barrichello slows on final straight to let Schumacher win.

Consequences

Public backlash; FIA bans team orders that affect race results.

Legacy

Team orders rule later repealed, but incident remains controversial.
