Spygate - 2007 Espionage Scandal

Summary

McLaren was found in possession of confidential Ferrari technical documents.

Key Figures Involved Mike Coughlan, Nigel Stepney, Ron Dennis

Timeline of Events

July 2007: Allegations arise; September 2007: McLaren fined $100 million and excluded from Constructors' Championship.

Consequences

Largest fine in F1 history; reputational damage to McLaren.

Legacy

Highlighted the importance of intellectual property in motorsport.
