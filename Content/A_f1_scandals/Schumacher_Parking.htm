Schumacher Parking – 2006 Monaco Grand Prix

Summary

Michael Schumacher controversially stopped his car at Rascasse during qualifying, allegedly to block rivals from setting faster laps.

Key Figures Involved Michael Schumacher, Fernando Alonso, Ferrari Team, FIA Stewards

Timeline of Events

  • May 27, 2006 – Qualifying session at Monaco.
  • Schumacher stops at Rascasse corner, bringing out yellow flags.
  • Alonso and others are unable to improve their lap times.
  • FIA investigates and strips Schumacher of his pole position.

Consequences

Schumacher was sent to the back of the grid. The incident damaged his reputation and was widely criticized as unsportsmanlike.

Legacy

The term 'Rascasse-gate' was coined. It remains a notorious example of gamesmanship in F1.
