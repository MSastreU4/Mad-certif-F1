Senna Disqualification - 1989 Japanese GP

Summary

Ayrton Senna was disqualified after a collision with Alain Prost and rejoining the track illegally.

Key Figures Involved Ayrton Senna, Alain Prost, Jean-Marie Balestre

Timeline of Events

October 1989: Collision at chicane; Senna wins but is disqualified post-race.

Consequences

Prost wins championship; Senna criticizes FIA bias.

Legacy

Fuelled one of F1's greatest rivalries and debates on FIA impartiality.
