Points Manipulation - 1997 Schumacher-Villeneuve

Summary

Schumacher collided with Villeneuve in the title-deciding race, attempting to take him out.

Key Figures Involved Michael Schumacher, Jacques Villeneuve

Timeline of Events

October 1997: Collision at Jerez; Schumacher disqualified from championship.

Consequences

First time a driver was excluded from the championship standings.

Legacy

Set precedent for penalizing unsportsmanlike conduct.
