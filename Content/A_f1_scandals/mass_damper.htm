Mass Damper Ban - 2006 Renault

Summary

Renault's mass damper system was banned mid-season despite initial approval.

Key Figures Involved Renault F1 Team, FIA

Timeline of Events

2006: FIA reverses approval; Renault forced to remove the system.

Consequences

Performance drop for Renault; controversy over rule interpretation.

Legacy

Highlighted inconsistency in technical regulation enforcement.
